How to Choose a Gaming Monitor

Key specs explained so you can pick the correct display.

Refresh Rate

Higher refresh rates (144Hz+) benefit competitive players.

Resolution

1440p is a great balance for modern GPUs; 4K is for top-tier rigs.

Panel Type

IPS for colors and viewing angles, TN for lowest latency, VA for contrast.

Response Time

Lower ms reduces ghosting; aim for 1–5 ms for gaming.

FAQs

Is 60Hz enough?
For casual players yes; competitive players prefer 120Hz+.
